CULTURE

Maracatu Nation: The Heartbeat of Carnival

Deep within Pernambuco's cultural tapestry lies Maracatu, an Afro-Brazilian performance art that combines music, dance, and ritual. Dating back to the 18th century, this tradition honors African kings and queens, maintaining its sacred roots while evolving into one of carnival's most spectacular attractions.
